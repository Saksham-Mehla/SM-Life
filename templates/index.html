How to Play

This is Cello.

Your task is to kill Cello using attacking patterns in fewest possible moves. The final objective is to try to do so in 1 move only.

Click in a cell to change its color.

  • Any black cell with exactly 3 white neighbors comes to life.
  • Any alive cell with less than 2 or more than 3 white neighbors turns to black.

When the game is paused, you can create different patterns of white cells in the grid. When you press play, these cells will interact with each other based on the above mentioned rules. Each time you click play is counted as one move. Depending on their arrangement, you can make a wide variety of patterns.

  • Glider : Travels till infinity

  • Stables : Once the form, they don't change

  • Oscillator : keeps changing back and forth

  • OR CREATE A NEW PATTERN...

Use the practice mode to try out new patterns for fun.

There. You are ready to play. All the best
